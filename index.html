Portfolio Projects & Case Studies

Automated Lead Boost

Contract-Based Project | 2024 Marketing Automation SaaS

Challenge: The sales team was spending 4-5 hours daily manually scraping LinkedIn leads into Excel spreadsheets, relying on outdated Fiverr lead lists that caused 30% email bounce rates. Their outreach was generating under 5% reply rates due to long LinkedIn messages and generic cold emails. The company needed to save time, improve lead quality, and significantly increase response rates and booked meetings.

Solution Implemented:

  • Built an automated AI lead generation system integrating Lusha with Apollo API and LinkedIn, eliminating manual scraping while improving data accuracy
  • Created a comprehensive ROI model demonstrating the value of monthly tool investment vs. manual labor costs
  • Transitioned outreach to Lemlist with email warming and domain rotation to minimize bounce rates
  • Developed short, natural messaging that generated higher replies and meeting bookings, avoiding generic sales copy
  • Created segmented HubSpot sequences with tailored messaging for different target demographics
  • Conducted two-week testing and optimization period ensuring all campaigns, workflows, and sequences functioned seamlessly before full launch

Measurable Results:

  • Freed 4-5 hours per week from manual lead scraping activities
  • Increased LinkedIn reply rate from 5% to 20%
  • Improved email reply rate from 3% to 15%
  • Boosted weekly meetings booked from 2-3 to 8-10 consistently
  • Reduced email bounce rate from 30% to under 5%

The Dead Lead Resurrection

Contract-Based Project | 2024 B2B SaaS Analytics Platform

Challenge: I inherited a disorganized CRM containing hundreds of leads marked as "Not Qualified," "No Response," or "Not Interested" from a previous SDR. Management suggested ignoring these leads, but many companies actually matched our ideal customer profile perfectly. The goal was to revive these dormant leads, generate meetings, and create new pipeline value.

Solution Implemented:

  • Exported and segmented dead leads by reason codes to understand root causes of previous failures
  • Verified and updated contact information using ZoomInfo while adding recent trigger events (funding, product launches, leadership changes)
  • Created a revival scoring system based on ICP fit and current business triggers to prioritize outreach efforts
  • Built custom messaging tracks addressing different objection types and previous interaction history
  • Implemented transparent re-engagement messages acknowledging previous interactions to create authentic, human connections

Measurable Results:

  • Revived 50+ conversations from "dead" leads within 3 months
  • Achieved 35% reply-to-meeting conversion rate (compared to 10% from cold outbound)
  • Generated $120K in qualified pipeline from previously inactive territory
  • Closed deals worth $45K Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

African Market Entry Experiment

Contract-Based Project | 2024 Payment Processing SaaS

Challenge: FlowCash wanted to expand into African markets, but no team members understood local business dynamics, cultural nuances, or market-specific challenges. Using Apollo-generated leads, I needed to adapt outreach strategies for each market and secure qualified meetings with pilot users.

Solution Implemented:

  • Conducted comprehensive market research using AI tools, Reddit discussions, and local business community groups
  • Rewrote all messaging to address region-specific challenges like forex volatility, mobile money integration, and local regulatory requirements
  • Tested multiple communication channels, including WhatsApp outreach in Kenya where it's the preferred business communication method
  • Targeted contacts with international company experience to facilitate easier relationship building and cultural bridge-building

Measurable Results:

  • Nigeria: 18% email reply rate, 12 qualified meetings, 5 pilot users signed
  • Kenya: 20% combined reply rate (email + WhatsApp), 10 meetings, 4 pilot users signed
  • South Africa: 15% reply rate, 8 meetings, 3 pilot users signed
  • Generated $40K in pilot revenue within 3 months
  • Created comprehensive sales playbook with cultural insights and best practices for future international expansion

The No-Show Problem Solver

Upwork Freelance Project | 2025 Sales AI & Territory Management Tool

Challenge: The client was booking approximately 10 meetings monthly, but 30% of prospects weren't showing up, and nearly half of attendees weren't properly qualified.
