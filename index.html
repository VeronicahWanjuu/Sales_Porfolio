My Systematic Sales Approach

Success in sales isn't luck‚Äîit's following a proven, repeatable process. Here's the 11-step system I use.

Step 1 | Deep Product Knowledge

I start by learning your product completely: what it does, how it works, competitive advantages, pricing, and customer success stories. I study what's working in your company by learning from top performing reps and copying successful strategies.

Step 2 | Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Research & Analysis

I do deep research into your target customers: company details, demographics, behavior patterns, buying cycles, decision making processes, and pain points. Understanding what keeps them awake at night helps me position solutions that connect.

Step 3 | Strategic List Building & Targeting

Using tools like Apollo, ZoomInfo, and LinkedIn Sales Navigator, I build clean, verified prospect lists based on ICP research. I continuously update and improve these lists, adding buyer signals and intent data to target the right people at the right time.

Step 4 | Lead Qualification and Scoring

I use proven frameworks like BANT and MEDDIC to score and qualify prospects based on fit, potential value, and readiness to buy. This ensures all outreach efforts focus on high conversion opportunities, making everything more efficient and profitable.

Step 5 | Message Creation & Testing

I create human, conversational messages that highlight specific gaps or opportunities for each prospect. Every message is tested through A/B campaigns to find what works best and drives the highest conversion rates.

Step 6 | Multi-Touch Outreach Campaigns

I run coordinated campaigns across LinkedIn, email, and phone, with smart follow-ups designed to start conversations, qualify interest, and move prospects through the funnel. Every touchpoint is tracked and improved based on engagement data.

Step 7 | Strategic Follow-Up Mastery

My follow-up system includes multiple touchpoints tailored to each prospect's position in their buying journey. I create both automated sequences and personalized outreach, following up consistently until getting a clear yes or no. No prospect gets forgotten.

Step 8 | Meeting Booking & Preparation

I book meetings with qualified decision makers and make sure they're properly prepared for success, resulting in high show-up rates and productive conversations for Account Executives.

Step 9 | Discovery Calls & Objection Handling

During calls, I focus on listening and asking smart questions that let prospects reach their own conclusions. I prevent objections through thorough discovery rather than handling them after they come up. When objections do happen, I use them as opportunities to understand deeper concerns and guide toward solutions.

Step 10 | Deal Closing

My approach centers on problem propositions, not just value propositions. Using proven methods and understanding of human psychology, I help prospects discover problems they didn't know they had, then show exactly how our solution addresses those specific issues.

Step 11 | Upselling & Referral Generation

I review CRM data to find opportunities with existing clients for additional services or expansions. I also systematically ask satisfied clients for referrals, expanding the pipeline and generating new high quality leads.

Continuous Improvement

Throughout this entire process, I constantly research new methods, test different approaches, and learn from top performing reps. I actively seek feedback and make data driven adjustments to improve results.

When you work with me, I treat your pipeline as my personal mission. I'm not just making calls. I'm following a proven system with relentless follow-up that connects people with solutions that genuinely improve their business.
